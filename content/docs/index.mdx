## Platform Overview

José Madrid Salsa is a full-featured e-commerce platform built with Next.js, featuring:

- **E-commerce Functionality** - Complete product catalog, shopping cart, and checkout
- **Payment Processing** - Multiple payment methods (Stripe, PayPal, Square)
- **Real-time Features** - Live notifications and order updates
- **Admin Dashboard** - Comprehensive management tools
- **Location Services** - Store locator with Google Maps integration
- **Automated Workflows** - Cron jobs and scheduled tasks
- **Lead Generation** - Advanced scraping and data collection
- **Email Marketing** - Automated email campaigns and templates
